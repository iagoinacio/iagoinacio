## 🕸️ ANANSE Business and Intelligence

**Ananse** is an AI-powered economic intelligence platform with immersive visual narrative — like the West African spider of lore, the AI weaves scattered data threads to reveal the bigger picture behind the noise.

### 📊 Economic Intelligence Dashboard

> Real-time monitoring of Brazil, continents, and geopolitical shocks.

| Module | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Macro Intelligence** | GDP, Inflation (IPCA), Selic, Unemployment, FX (USD/BRL), Ibovespa — 19+ typed indicators with live data |
| **Geo-economic Radar** | Monitoring 5 continents: growth, inflation, interest rates, country risk & global pressure |
| **Cognitive Engine (AI)** | Hybrid narratives generated by statistical models + LLMs with explainability and transparent governance |
| **Economic Climate** | Simplified daily thermometer: pocket impact, personalized alerts, consumption & credit recommendations |
| **Investment Playbook** | Stocks, currencies, crypto, fixed income & commodities monitor with risk-profiled recommendations (conservative → dynamic) |
| **Macro Briefing** | Guided macro scenario reading with short-term signals and geopolitical vectors |

### 🔌 Integrated Data Sources

`BCB` · `World Bank` · `IBGE SIDRA` · `Ipeadata` · `Comex Stat` · `National Treasury` · `UNHCR` · `dados.gov.br` · `Eurostat` · `NewsAPI`

> Live coverage: ~83% of indicators | Transparent fallback for unavailable data

### 🤖 AI Integration

Conversational economic analysis engine powered by **[Pollinations.ai](https://pollinations.ai/)** — free for all users. Connect your account and chat with Ananse's intelligence engine.
